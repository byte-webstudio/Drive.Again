About us

Welcome to DRIVE AGAIN, your trusted partner in automotive service and dealership established in 2025.

Located near Panakkathottam building materials at Siruvani junction in Edakurussi, Karimba, we are committed to getting you back on the road with confidence by offering premium, professional services right at your doorstep. Our expert team specializes in comprehensive mechanical and electrical works, professional painting and bodywork, meticulous vehicle detailing, and full restoration services. Furthermore, we handle all necessary paperworks and offer convenient vehicle pickup and delivery to ensure a seamless experience for you, with 24/7 service available to meet your needs.
